About Us

Our Problem

The transist system was extremely uneffective and didn't have the stops people wanted!

Many routes did not go to the spots that people wanted and the carbon foot print of so many cars was damaging our once pure earth!

New Design

We wanted to allow people to make new routes and have a say of what the start and end of a trip should be and the many end points in between!

We wanted people to feel that a car is not always necessary and then a bus could be just as effective

Our Solution

Reduce the amount of cars on the streets by having a more effective public transportation system

Help reduce our carbon footprint by reducing the amount of cars
